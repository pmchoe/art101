Challenge

Brushing Your Teeth for Fools 101

  • Grab your toothbrush
  • Grab your toothpaste
    • Open/Remove the toothpaste cap
  • Put toothpaste on your toothbrush
    • Pea-sized amount
  • Put toothbrush into your mouth
  • Start brushing
    • Move toothbrush in circular motion with bristles facing the teeth
    • 1-2 minutes of brush time
      • At least 30 seconds on left and right side of teeth
  • Spit out remaining toothpaste in mouth
    • Wipe lips and surrounding area of toothpaste
  • Put back toothpaste
  • Put back toothbrush

Rock Paper Scissors Pseudocode

		Display a welcome message and game instructions.
		Initialize variables
			player_score = 0
			computer_score = 0
			rounds_played = 0
			choices = ["rock", "paper", "scissors"]
		Repeat the following until the player chooses to quit:
		Prompt the player to choose one of the following options:
			"Rock"
			"Paper"
			"Scissors"
			"quit" (to exit the game)
		Generate a random choice for the computer from the "choices" list.
		Display the player's choice and the computer's choice.
		Compare the choices to determine the round winner:
		If player's choice == computer's choice, it's a tie.
		If (player's choice == "rock" and computer's choice == "scissors") or (player's choice == "paper" and computer's choice == "rock") or (player's choice == "scissors" and computer's choice == "paper"), then the player wins the round. 
			Otherwise, the computer wins the round. 
		Update the scores accordingly: 
			If the player wins, increment player_score by 1.
			If the computer wins, increment computer_score by 1.
		Display the round result (tie, player wins, or computer wins).
		Increment rounds_played by 1.
		Ask the player if they want to play another round or quit.
		Display the final game result, including the player's score, computer's score, and the number of rounds played.
		Thank the player for playing and then exit the game.
